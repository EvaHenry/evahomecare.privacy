Eva Homecare Agency Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/1/2016
1. Purpose
    This Privacy Policy explains how Eva Homecare Agency collects, uses, stores, and protects information received through messages sent by employees. This includes messages related to scheduling, patient care coordination, payroll, compliance, emergencies, and general work communication.
2. Scope
    This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and caregivers who communicate with the Company through:
        SMS or text messages.
        Messaging apps (e.g., WhatsApp, Microsoft Teams).
        Company communication platforms or mobile applications.
        Email or voicemail messages.
3. Information Collected
    When employees send messages to the Company, we may collect:
        Name and employee ID.
        Phone number or email address.
        Message content (including text, images, or attachments).
        Date, time, and delivery status of messages.
        Work-related information such as schedules, availability, or incident reports.
    We do not request unnecessary personal information through messaging.
4. How We Use Employee Messages
    Messages are used strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:
        Scheduling and shift coordination.
        Patient care communication.
        Payroll and attendance verification.
        Compliance, quality assurance, and training.
        Health, safety, or emergency situations.
        Internal investigations or legal obligations.
    Messages are not used for marketing or sold to third parties.
5. Confidentiality & Patient Information
    Employees must not send unnecessary patient-identifiable information unless required for care coordination and permitted by law. Any patient information received through messages is handled in accordance with HIPAA and applicable healthcare privacy laws.
6. Data Storage & Retention
    Messages may be stored securely on company systems or approved third-party platforms.
    Access is limited to authorized personnel only.
    Messages are retained only as long as necessary to meet operational, legal, or regulatory requirements.
7. Data Sharing
    Employee messages may be shared:
        Internally with management, HR, payroll, or compliance teams as needed.
        With regulatory authorities if legally required.
        With secure service providers who support company operations.
    We do not share employee messages with unauthorized third parties.
8. Security Measures
    We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect message data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Employees are expected to protect their devices and report any suspected data breach immediately.
9. Employee Responsibilities
    Employees are responsible for:
        Using messaging systems professionally and appropriately.
        Avoiding sharing sensitive personal or patient data unless necessary.
        Reporting lost or compromised devices.
        Following company communication and data protection policies.
10. Monitoring & Compliance
    The Company reserves the right to monitor, review, and retain messages for business, legal, or compliance purposes. Employees should have no expectation of personal privacy when using company-approved communication channels for work-related messaging.
11. Employee Rights
    Employees may request access to their stored message data or request corrections where applicable, subject to legal and operational limitations.
12. Policy Updates
    This policy may be updated periodically. Employees will be notified of material changes.
13. Contact Information
    For questions or concerns regarding this policy, contact:
        HR Department
        hr@evahomecare.com
